" Die Luft der Freiheit weht !!! "

"the winds of freedom blow" Welcome to Stanford University, where tradition meets innovation at the intersection of excellence and opportunity. Founded in 1885 by Leland and Jane Stanford, we are proud to be one of the world’s leading institutions for education, research, and entrepreneurship. Our beautiful 8,180-acre campus is home to a vibrant community of students, faculty, and staff who are passionate about making a global impact. At Stanford, we foster an environment where groundbreaking research and learning thrive. Our world-class programs in a variety of disciplines—from engineering and technology to humanities and social sciences—empower students to explore, innovate, and lead. Here, you'll find a collaborative spirit that drives creativity and a commitment to solving the world’s most pressing challenges. As the birthplace of countless successful startups and home to some of the brightest minds across all fields, Stanford has been instrumental in shaping the future of technology, medicine, and business. We are proud to have nurtured entrepreneurs like Larry Page and Sergey Brin, whose work revolutionized the way we connect, communicate, and access information. Join us at Stanford, where the pursuit of knowledge, innovation, and social good never stops. Together, we will continue to make a lasting impact on the world.
